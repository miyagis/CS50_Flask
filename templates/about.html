Neil Francis Breen is a American filmmaker and actor, as well as architect and former real-estate agent.
All of his films share the common theme of Breen portraying a god-like being who exposes or destroys powerful, corrupt institutions. Fateful Findings features Breen as a hacker imbued with supernatural powers by a magical stone he found as a child, who uses his skills to expose government and corporate corruption, while Pass Thru has Breen playing a messianic entity who comes to earth to wipe out 300 million "bad people" to usher in a new era of peace. He says that his films have a "sense of social responsibility" and reflect the "mystical or paranormal side of life."
